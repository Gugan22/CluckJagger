Growth Promoters

Various modern growth promoting strategies and suitable disease preventive measures plays a major role in growth in poultry industry. The various essential growth promoters and novel feed supplements and bioactive principles are used for the improving poultry production and health. Apart from boosting poultry production and safeguarding general health of birds some of these have been found to possess beneficial immunomodulatory and stress relieving properties and also have added advantages to help produce lean meat and designer poultry products taking into account health awareness and preferences of the consumers. A perspective vision and holistic usages of growth promoters will give beneficial applications in poultry production system for better economic growth.

Antibiotics

Antibiotic Growth Promoters (AGP) involve the use of antibiotics in poultry feed to improve overall growth and feed conversion. AGPs help improve the flockâ€™s performance and keeps their health in check. It promotes growth by reducing growth-suppressing metabolites caused by microbes. These growth promoters also increase amino acid levels in the gut, and reduces stress and mortality in birds.

Probiotics

These are live microbial feed supplements which are used for balancing the population of microbes in the intestines. It restrains the growth of disease producing microbes in the birds, prevents digestive problems, enables absorption of nutrients, improves feed intake, increases growth rate and body weight, boosts fertility, reduces stress, and performs various other functions to enhance growth.

Prebiotics

Prebiotics beneficially affect the host by stimulating growth of harmless bacteria. It provides energy and other limited nutrients to the intestinal mucosa. Probiotics also work by hindering the colonization of pathogenic bacteria in the birds.
